💡 What is Tusi

Tusi helps you translate text quickly. It sits in your macOS menu bar. You click the icon, type your text, and get the translation. It detects the language for you so you do not have to select it. Tusi uses your own API keys to provide translations.

✨ Key Features

  • • Easy access: The app lives in your menu bar.
  • • Auto detection: The app finds the language for you.
  • • Private usage: You bring your own API keys to keep data secure.
  • • Fast setup: You start translating in minutes.

📥 How to install Tusi

Follow these steps to get Tusi working on your computer:

  1. Visit the official download page to find the latest version.
  2. Click the download link for the installer package.
  3. Open the file once the download finishes.
  4. Drag the Tusi icon into your Applications folder.
  5. Double-click the Tusi icon in your Applications folder to launch the app.

⚙️ Setting up the app

Tusi requires an API key to function. The app is compatible with various AI services. When you open Tusi, you will see a settings window. Enter your API key from OpenAI or DeepSeek into the box. Save your changes to bridge your connection. Once saved, Tusi stays ready in your menu bar at all times.

🖥️ System Requirements

Tusi works on any modern Mac computer. Ensure your operating system is up to date to avoid issues. The app uses very little memory so it stays quiet in the background while you perform your work.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay a subscription? Tusi is free to download. You only pay the provider of your API key based on how much you use the translation feature.

Where does my data go? Your text goes directly from your computer to the translation service you picked. Tusi does not store your content on our own servers.
